Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid-colored business attire that aligns with your industry standards. Avoid busy patterns or bright colors that might distract from your face. Bring 2-3 outfit options to ensure variety in your shots.

How long does a session take?

Individual sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and wardrobe changes. For team bookings, we can accommodate up to 20 people per day with efficient 15-minute time slots.

When will I receive my photos?

Standard delivery is within 3 business days. Rush delivery is available within 24 hours for an additional fee. You'll receive both high-resolution files and web-optimized versions for digital use.

Do you offer hair and makeup services?

Yes, we partner with professional makeup artists and hair stylists who specialize in camera-ready looks. This service can be added to any package for an additional fee.

What about licensing and usage rights?

All packages include full commercial usage rights. You're free to use your images across all marketing channels, social media, websites, and printed materials without restrictions or additional fees.
